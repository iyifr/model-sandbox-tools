# Case Notes — Smith v. Acme Corp.

**Client:** Jane Smith  
**Date of incident:** March 15, 2025  
**Case type:** Employment discrimination  

## Summary

Jane Smith was terminated from Acme Corp. on March 20, 2025, after twelve years of employment as a Senior Analyst. Ms. Smith alleges the termination was retaliatory, occurring two weeks after she filed an internal HR complaint regarding disparate treatment by her direct supervisor, Robert Chen.

## Key Facts

- Ms. Smith received consistently positive performance reviews from 2018 through 2024.
- On March 1, 2025, she submitted a formal complaint to HR documenting exclusion from team meetings and denial of promotion opportunities.
- Mr. Chen issued a written warning on March 10, 2025, citing "communication issues" without prior verbal counseling.
- Termination letter dated March 20, 2025, cited "restructuring" despite Acme posting two Senior Analyst openings the following week.

## Relief Sought

Reinstatement with back pay, compensatory damages, and injunctive relief requiring Acme to implement anti-retaliation training.
